About

I'm an independent philosophical researcher developing a body of work on finite description, mediated judgment, correction, and decision systems under constraint. My papers focus on how models, representations, classifications, and formal systems make parts of reality intelligible and actionable while never fully exhausting what they govern. This site collects my current papers, drafts, and related work as part of an ongoing effort to build clearer frameworks for understanding science, institutions, and consequence-bearing systems.
